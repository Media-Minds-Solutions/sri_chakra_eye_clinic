The Vision Behind Sri Chakra

Founded in 2008 with a profound vision to transform eye care in Anantapur, Sri Chakra Eye Clinic emerged from a simple yet powerful belief: every person deserves access to world-class ophthalmology, regardless of their location or background.

The name "Sri Chakra" symbolizes the ancient concept of enlightenment through clarity of vision - both literal and metaphorical. Just as the chakra represents the wheel of energy and balance, our clinic serves as a center of healing energy, restoring not just sight but hope and quality of life to our patients.

What began as a modest practice has grown into a comprehensive eye care center, serving over 50,000 patients from Anantapur and surrounding districts. Our journey reflects our unwavering commitment to bringing advanced medical technology and compassionate care to a region that was traditionally underserved by specialized healthcare facilities.

"Our mission has always been simple: to be the beacon of hope for those seeking clarity in their vision and peace in their hearts."

Advanced Technology & Equipment

State-of-the-art diagnostic and surgical equipment ensuring precise, safe, and effective treatments

OCT Imaging

Optical Coherence Tomography provides detailed cross-sectional images of the retina, enabling early detection of macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, and glaucoma.

Phaco Surgery

Advanced phacoemulsification system for minimally invasive cataract surgery with faster recovery times and superior visual outcomes.

Digital Fundus

High-resolution retinal photography for comprehensive documentation and monitoring of retinal conditions and diabetic eye disease.

Surgical Microscope

Precision surgical microscope with advanced optics ensuring accurate and safe microsurgical procedures for complex eye conditions.

Auto Refraction

Automated refraction system for precise measurement of refractive errors, ensuring accurate prescription for glasses and contact lenses.

Tonometry

Non-contact tonometer for comfortable and accurate measurement of intraocular pressure, crucial for glaucoma screening and monitoring.

Culturally Rooted, Globally Advanced

At Sri Chakra Eye Clinic, we understand that healthcare is deeply personal and cultural. Our approach honors the rich traditions of Andhra Pradesh while delivering internationally recognized medical standards.

Our multilingual team communicates fluently in Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, and English, ensuring every patient feels comfortable and understood. We respect traditional health beliefs while gently educating about modern treatment options.

Our Cultural Commitments:

  • Multilingual consultations in patient's preferred language
  • Respect for traditional health practices and beliefs
  • Family-centered care approach honoring local customs
  • Flexible scheduling for agricultural and festival seasons
