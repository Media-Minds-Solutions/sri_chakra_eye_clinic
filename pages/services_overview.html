Retinal Care

Diabetic Retinopathy Specialist

Specialized treatment for diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and retinal disorders. Critical care for the high diabetic population in Anantapur region.

Regional Health Alert

Andhra Pradesh has one of the highest diabetes rates in India. Regular retinal screening is essential for diabetic patients to prevent vision loss. We recommend annual check-ups for all diabetic patients over 30.
