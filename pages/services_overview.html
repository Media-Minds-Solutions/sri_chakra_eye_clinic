Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my eyes checked?
Adults should get an eye check-up every 1–2 years. Children and people with diabetes, glaucoma, or family history of eye diseases should have more frequent check-ups.
What are the early signs of eye problems?
Blurred vision, eye pain, headaches, double vision, sudden flashes or floaters, difficulty seeing at night, or frequent squinting may indicate eye problems.
Can watching TV or using mobile for long hours damage my eyes?
Prolonged screen time causes digital eye strain and dryness. It doesn’t cause permanent damage but can worsen existing vision issues. Following the 20-20-20 rule helps.
What is the right age for a child’s first eye test?
Children should have an eye test by age 1 for screening and a full exam around 3–5 years. Early detection prevents long-term vision problems.
Does diet affect my eyesight?
Yes. Eating carrots, green leafy vegetables, fish rich in omega-3, and vitamin A rich foods supports eye health and reduces risk of night blindness.
Can wearing glasses weaken my eyesight?
No, wearing glasses does not weaken eyesight. They correct vision and reduce strain but do not change eye power permanently.
Are sunglasses necessary in Anantapur’s sunny climate?
Yes, UV-protective sunglasses are important in Anantapur to prevent cataracts, pterygium, and retinal damage from strong sunlight.
What is cataract and how is it treated?
Cataract is clouding of the natural lens. It is treated with surgery where the lens is replaced with an artificial intraocular lens (IOL).
What is glaucoma?
Glaucoma is damage to the optic nerve, usually due to high eye pressure. It can cause irreversible vision loss if not treated early.
What are the symptoms of glaucoma?
In early stages, glaucoma has no symptoms. Later, it may cause blurred vision, halos, eye pain, headaches, or tunnel vision.
What is diabetic retinopathy?
Diabetic retinopathy is an eye complication of diabetes that damages retinal blood vessels and may cause vision loss if untreated.
Can diabetes and high BP affect eyesight?
Yes, uncontrolled diabetes and hypertension can damage the retina, leading to vision loss, diabetic retinopathy, or hypertensive retinopathy.
What is conjunctivitis (red eye) and is it contagious?
Conjunctivitis, also called eye flu, causes redness, irritation, and watering. It is contagious and spreads through touch and sharing items.
What is dry eye and why is it common in Anantapur?
Dry eye occurs when tears are insufficient. Anantapur’s hot, dry climate increases risk of dryness, irritation, and eye fatigue.
What is squint (crossed eyes) and can it be treated?
Squint can be treated with glasses, exercises, or surgery depending on severity. Early treatment prevents lazy eye in children.
What is night blindness?
Night blindness is difficulty seeing in dim light, often due to vitamin A deficiency or retinal disorders.
Is cataract surgery safe?
Yes, modern cataract surgery is very safe, quick (10–15 minutes), and usually performed as a day procedure with fast recovery.
What is LASIK / laser surgery?
LASIK is a laser procedure to correct myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism, reducing or eliminating the need for glasses or contact lenses.
Who is eligible for LASIK surgery?
People above 18 years with stable power for at least one year and healthy corneas are usually eligible for LASIK surgery.
Is eye surgery painful?
Most modern eye surgeries, including cataract and LASIK, are painless due to anesthesia and advanced technology.
How long does it take to recover after cataract surgery?
Most patients recover within a few days. Vision stabilizes in 2–4 weeks after cataract surgery.
What precautions should I take after eye surgery?
After eye surgery, use prescribed drops, avoid rubbing eyes, protect from dust, wear sunglasses, and attend follow-ups.
Can children wear contact lenses?
Yes, children can wear contact lenses after 13–14 years under doctor’s guidance. Hygiene and proper care are essential.
Is it safe to sleep while wearing contact lenses?
No, sleeping with contact lenses increases the risk of serious eye infections and should be avoided.
Do computer glasses help with eye strain?
Yes, blue-light blocking or anti-glare computer glasses help reduce digital eye strain caused by long screen hours.
Do you provide eye check-ups for schools and companies in Anantapur?
Yes, we provide eye screening services for schools, colleges, and corporate offices in Anantapur.
Is emergency eye care available at your clinic?
Yes, we provide emergency eye care for injuries, infections, sudden vision loss, and foreign body removal.
Do you accept Aarogyasri health scheme?
Yes, we accept Aarogyasri and major health insurance for cataract surgery and other eligible procedures.
Do you provide treatment for children’s eye problems?
Yes, we have pediatric ophthalmology services for children’s vision care including squint, lazy eye, and refractive errors.

You can call our clinic, book online through our website, or walk in during working hours to schedule an appointment.
